About JetCast

Founded by SB Designs (Sebastian) and Sean FH (Sean) in 2025, JetCast is an aviation-themed podcast by two aviation enthusiasts! We operate as a talkshow and podcast, discussing the latest aviation news, events, or even history, and the different parts that make up flying and the aviation world! Tune in to learn more about the topic of aviation!
